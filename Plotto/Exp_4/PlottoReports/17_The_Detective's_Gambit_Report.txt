## Critical Assessment
"The Detective's Gambit" presents a well-crafted noir-inspired narrative with a compelling protagonist whose moral journey forms the emotional core of the story. The narrative effectively balances external detective work with internal ethical dilemmas, creating tension that drives the plot forward. The character development is strong, particularly for Isaac, whose past as a gambler informs his present choices in satisfying ways. The story's structure follows classic detective fiction conventions while incorporating contemporary elements of corporate espionage and intellectual property theft.

* **Develop Layla's character more fully**: As the antagonist, Layla would be more compelling with additional dimensionâ€”perhaps a glimpse of her own motivations or backstory that explains (without justifying) her theft of Paige's work.
