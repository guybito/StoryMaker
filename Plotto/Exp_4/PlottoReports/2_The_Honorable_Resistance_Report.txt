## Critical Assessment
"The Honorable Resistance" presents a compelling moral dilemma with a well-developed protagonist whose internal conflict drives a meaningful character arc. The story excels in creating high-stakes tension between duty and conscience, with strong character relationships that facilitate the protagonist's growth. The prose is clean and effective, with a clear narrative structure that builds to a satisfying, if somewhat idealistic, resolution. The military setting is convincingly portrayed, though some operational details could be more thoroughly developed to enhance realism.
