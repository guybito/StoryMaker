## Suggestions for Improvement:

* **Strengthen logical consistency**: The language barrier between Gabriel and the tribe is inadequately addressed. Developing a more plausible communication system would enhance believability.

* **Develop the artifact subplot**: The golden idol is introduced as significant but its importance fades. Either develop this element more fully or reduce its initial prominence to avoid creating unresolved questions.

* **Add more sensory detail**: While the narrative moves efficiently, key settings like the island and tribal village would benefit from richer sensory descriptions to immerse readers more fully in Gabriel's experience.

* **Smooth transitions between major plot points**: Some significant developments (like the mutiny and finding the island) feel rushed. Expanding these transitions would create a more balanced narrative rhythm.

* **Clarify Max's character and motivations**: Max shifts from mysterious recruiter to betrayer to ally. A clearer character arc for him would strengthen the overall narrative and make his possession of the radio feel less convenient.
