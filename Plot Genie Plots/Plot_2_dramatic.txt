In this story set in court, our hero is a sports editor who falls in love with a nurse...
Their goal is blocked by a major problem: desired accomplishment opposed by friends.
However, love does not come easy because the lover and beloved are rival leaders.
Things become more tangled when mental derangement threatened by a kinsman,
and matters worsen as there is a threatened strike.
At the height of tension, a crisis hits: fortune is threatened by a strike.
The story climaxes in a twist where wherein the victimizer becomes a victim of his own scheme
