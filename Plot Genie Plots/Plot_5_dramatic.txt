Genre: Dramatic
In this story set in a savage country, our hero is a auto mechanic who falls in love with a school teacher...
Their goal is blocked by a major problem: obliged to identify an unknown person opposed by inclement weather..
However, love does not come easy because the match is opposed by children of one.
Things become more tangled when fatal ambition threatens to deprive one of health,
and matters worsen as sacrifice to passion, habit or mania endangers life.
At the height of tension, a crisis hits: banishment of a loved one is threatened.
The story climaxes in a twist where in which evidence supposedly lost is recovered
