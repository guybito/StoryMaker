Genre: Dramatic
In this story set in a mansion, our hero is a man-about-town who falls in love with a seamstress...
Their goal is blocked by a major problem: desired information opposed by danger to life.
However, love does not come easy because lover required to prevent a catastrophe.
Things become more tangled when an illicit love affair or adultery threatens loss of health,
and matters worsen as there is a threatened class war.
At the height of tension, a crisis hits: about to be obliged to blackmail an unrecognized loved one.
The story climaxes in a twist where a chief witness against the hero or principals is found to be insane
