In this story set at a country club, our hero is a newsboy who falls in love with a cabaret entertainer...
Their goal is blocked by a major problem: relief by pursuit opposed by enemies.
However, love does not come easy because beloved spurns the love of lover.
Things become more tangled when there is a rivalry between unequals for love,
and matters worsen as duty to a loved one stands in the way of happiness.
At the height of tension, a crisis hits: there is a threatened mutiny.
