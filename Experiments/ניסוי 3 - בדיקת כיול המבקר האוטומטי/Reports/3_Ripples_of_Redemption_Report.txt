## Summary Report:
"Ripples of Redemption" is a well-crafted narrative with strong character development and compelling multi-layered conflict. The story effectively balances personal and community stakes while exploring themes of redemption, class conflict, and the consequences of long-held grudges. The prose is clear and accessible, with generally strong descriptive elements, though some plot developments rely on convenient timing. Overall, this is a polished, emotionally resonant story with a satisfying character arc and resolution.

## Suggestions for Improvement:

* **Plot Credibility**: Strengthen the plausibility of Harrison's decades-long vendetta and Vivian's convenient discoveries to make the story's revelations feel more organic and less contrived.
  
* **Scene Development**: Expand key emotional moments, particularly the confrontation scenes, with more sensory details and "showing" rather than "telling" to increase reader immersion and impact.
  
* **Supporting Characters**: Develop the clinic director and community members more fully to strengthen the community stakes and provide additional perspectives on the central conflict.
  
* **Pacing**: Tighten the exposition, particularly in the backstory sections and the courtroom revelation, to maintain narrative momentum throughout the story.
  
* **Thematic Integration**: More explicitly connect Elijah's past accident to his current moral dilemmas to strengthen the thematic throughline about responsibility and redemption.
