* **Deepen internal conflict**: While Margaret's external actions are well-portrayed, her internal struggles could be more explicitly developed. Show more of her moments of doubt or fear to heighten the emotional impact of her ultimate decisions.

* **Develop antagonist complexity**: Joshua and Isaac could be given more nuanced motivations beyond their roles as government agents. This would create a more morally complex narrative where readers understand the opposing viewpoint.

* **Clarify the ending implications**: The final scene could more explicitly address what the future holds for Margaret and James, giving readers a clearer sense of the long-term consequences of their choices.
