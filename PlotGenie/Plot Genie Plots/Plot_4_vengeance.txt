In this story set on the high seas, our hero is a artist who falls in love with a novelist...
Their goal is blocked by a major problem: obliged to risk happiness in an effort to brave a maniac or insane person..
However, love does not come easy because lover has sworn vengeance against kin of beloved.
Things become more tangled when revenge is sought against an immortal for having brought loss of riches,
and matters worsen as kidnapping is threatened by parties desiring revenge.
At the height of tension, a crisis hits: disaster is threatened by the vengeance of a kinsman.
The story climaxes in a twist where in which the revenge plot of the enemy proves to be a blessing to the hero
