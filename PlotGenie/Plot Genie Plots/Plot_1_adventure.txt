In this story set in the alps, our hero is a poacher who falls in love with a hotel hostess...
Their goal is blocked by a major problem: a mutiny is threatened by physical suffering.
However, love does not come easy because lover accused of deception as to his wealth.
Things become more tangled when there is a rivalry between kinsmen for love,
and matters worsen as sacrifice to passion, habit or mania threatens loss of reward.
At the height of tension, a crisis hits: disaster is threatened by fire.
The story climaxes in a twist where wherein the actions of a character have all along been misunderstood, and the truth comes out in logical sequence
