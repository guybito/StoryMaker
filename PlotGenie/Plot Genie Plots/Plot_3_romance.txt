In this story set in a canyon, our hero is a magnate who falls in love with a editor...
Their goal is blocked by a major problem: obliged to restore health or happiness to a loved one opposed by poverty..
However, love does not come easy because kinsman of beloved is possessed with fatal ambition for power.
Things become more tangled when an illicit love affair threatens loss of achievement to a loved one,
and matters worsen as kidnapping is threatened by rivals.
At the height of tension, a crisis hits: loss of sight, hearing, or other senses of a loved one is threatened.
The story climaxes in a twist where wherein the enemy is suddenly stricken with fatal illness
