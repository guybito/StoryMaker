In this story set on a train, our hero is a magician who falls in love with a censor...
Their goal is blocked by a major problem: relief from persecution opposed by enemies.
However, love does not come easy because lover possesses fatal ambition for fame.
Things become more tangled when fatal indiscretion threatens loss of liberty,
and matters worsen as an innocent person is accused of having committed a murder.
At the height of tension, a crisis hits: learn that a loved one is a murderer.
The story climaxes in a twist where a loved one feigns illness or death in order to secure information necessary for victory
