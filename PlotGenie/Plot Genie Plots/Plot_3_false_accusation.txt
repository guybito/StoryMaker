In this story set in hawaii, our hero is a squatter who falls in love with a diver...
Their goal is blocked by a major problem: obliged to prevent a catastrophe opposed by poverty..
However, love does not come easy because lover falsely accused of being socially unfit.
Things become more tangled when remorse threatens a sacrifice of achievement by a loved one,
and matters worsen as an innocent person is accused of having cheated.
At the height of tension, a crisis hits: banishment is threatened.
The story climaxes in a twist where wherein an officer of the enemy knows the hero to be innocent and allows him to escape
